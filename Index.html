Dr. Aklilu Lemma

Discovered a natural treatment to bilharzia

description of the person

  • 1935 --Born in Jijiga,Ethiopia
  • 1964 --He made his important discovery. Which was a natural treatment to schistosomiasis, also known as Bilharzia
  • 1976 --Started a job in United nation. where he worked in different positions as a scienties and then also as deputy director of UNICEF's international child development center.
  • 1989 --He won The Right Liivelihood Award of Sweden together with his research associate Dr.Legesse Wolde-Yohannes.And many more awards.
  • 1997 --He died in United States and was buried in his home country Ethiopia
